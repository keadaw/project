Studio Ltd.


Who are we and what do we do?


Studio designs and builds intelligent, cost-effective and intuitive web applications, cloud systems, desktop applications and mobile apps that help streamline processes for businesses as well as create new revenue streams for start-ups and established businesses alike. With offices in Farnborough (Hampshire), London, Manchester, Madrid, Seville and Bucharest, Studio is the UK’s leading bespoke software development company, with a fully in-house 300+ team of expert software developers and technology professionals specialising in both Microsoft and the MEAN stack.

Consultation


As a software development company, we have a clear objective: to create innovative software to elevate client business. Studio is on a mission to re-inspire what business software looks like and how it can more than pay for itself. The hallmark of a Studio delivery is client success. Whether through genuine consultancy, overseeing the evolution of an existing system or building a business interface from its infancy, we are invested in our clients' growth. Studio is the catalyst to unlock potential for all types of businesses in the UK and across Europe. Studio was founded to repeatedly deliver transformative software, on time and on budget. Our mission is to solve the gap between expectation and delivery. - to make software work for the user - not the other way round. We deliver this through genuine consultancy and retained development expertise.

Overview - How we do it


We operate a two fold process.


The ‘design’ phase ensures that we create a complete specification. The visual blueprint offer all parties - owners, staff team members a clear working picture of what the end product will look like. The design exploration would analyse the full requirements of a project, from the technology and structure to the design and user experience. We use an Agile methodology to progress the design phase.

The 'build' phase is a streamlined process where our specialist developers takes the client from the design and plan to the delivery of a perfectly executed product.
